About Neelam mangos

The Neelam mango possesses several distinctive features that contribute to its special status among mango varieties. One of its key specialties is the deliciously sweet and aromatic taste that sets it apart. The mango's flavor profile is a harmonious blend of sweetness with subtle undertones of citrus, creating a delightful experience for mango enthusiasts. Its smooth, non-fibrous flesh enhances the eating quality, making Neelam mango a preferred choice for those who appreciate a velvety texture in their fruit.

Neelam mango is primarily found in South India, with Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka being significant regions for its cultivation. The mango orchards in these states provide an ideal environment for Neelam mango to flourish, thanks to the warm climate and suitable soil conditions. The cultivation of Neelam mango has expanded beyond its native regions, and it is also grown in other parts of India with favorable climates.The mango season for Neelam typically aligns with the warmer months, and during this time, the fruit is readily available in local markets, adding to the diverse array of mango varieties enjoyed during the summer season.
