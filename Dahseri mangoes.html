Dasheri mango, scientifically known as Mangifera indica, is a popular mango variety cultivated mainly in the Northern Indian states, particularly in the Malihabad region of Uttar Pradesh. Known for its distinct sweet aroma, vibrant yellow skin with red blush, and deliciously juicy flesh, the Dasheri mango is a favorite among mango enthusiasts. About Dahseri Mangoes

Beyond its visual appeal, the true essence of Dasheri mango lies in its delectable taste and texture. The fruit's juicy flesh boasts a perfect balance of sweetness and a subtle hint of tanginess, creating a flavor profile that is both indulgent and refreshing. Dasheri mangoes are a popular choice for consumption during the mango season, and their rich taste elevates them to a favored status among mango enthusiasts. The Dasheri mango holds a myriad of distinctive qualities that contribute to its esteemed status among mango aficionados. One of its standout features is the enchanting sweet aroma that envelops the surroundings when the fruit is ripe, creating a sensory experience that is as delightful as its taste.

Dasheri mango, a renowned variety, is primarily cultivated in the Malihabad region of Uttar Pradesh, Northern India. This region, with its favorable climate and soil conditions, provides an ideal environment for the growth of Dasheri mangoes. The geographical significance of Malihabad in the cultivation of Dasheri mangoes highlights the specificity of the region's conditions that contribute to the unique characteristics of this mango variety. The orchards in Uttar Pradesh benefit from the region's tropical climate, with warm temperatures and distinct seasons, allowing for the optimal maturation of the fruit.
